# Assets License and Attribution

This document provides attribution for all third-party assets used in SNEJK2D.

## Graphics

### Parallax Backgrounds
All parallax backgrounds created by **Ansimuz** and available on itch.io:

- **Forest** (`backgrounds/forest/`)
  - Source: [Parallax Forest](https://ansimuz.itch.io/parallax-forest)
  - Layers: back-trees, middle-trees, front-trees, lights

- **Mountain Dusk** (`backgrounds/mountain/`)
  - Source: [Mountain Dusk Parallax Background](https://ansimuz.itch.io/mountain-dusk-parallax-background)
  - Layers: sky, far-clouds, near-clouds, far-mountains, mountains, trees

- **Cyberpunk Street** (`backgrounds/cyberpunk/`)
  - Source: [Cyberpunk Street Environment](https://ansimuz.itch.io/cyberpunk-street-environment)
  - Layers: far-buildings, back-buildings, foreground

- **City Night** (`backgrounds/city/`)
  - Source: [Cyberpunk Street Environment](https://ansimuz.itch.io/cyberpunk-street-environment)
  - Layers: back, middle, foreground, foreground-empty

- **Country Platform** (`backgrounds/country/`)
  - Source: [Country Side Platfformer](https://ansimuz.itch.io/country-side-platfformer)
  - Includes: platform tileset and forest scenery

- **Underwater** (`backgrounds/underwater/`)
  - Source: [Underwater Fantasy Pixel Art Environment](https://ansimuz.itch.io/underwater-fantasy-pixel-art-environment)
  - Layers: far, foreground-1, foreground-2, foregound-merged, sand

**License**: Free for personal and commercial use
**Attribution**: Art by Ansimuz (https://ansimuz.itch.io/)
